About Me

Welcome to my Fall Classes page! Here's a little bit about me:

My name is Elizabeth Gill and I’m a senior at Virginia Tech, majoring in advertising with a minor in professional and technical writing. Though I was born in Newark, New Jersey, I grew up in Gloucester, Virginia. I wholeheartedly claim Virginia as my home. Since childhood, I’ve loved creative work, from writing stories to planning theatrical scenes. Advertising allows me to combine my passions for communication and art. Outside of my professional and academic career, I enjoy live music; collecting oddities; trying new foods; watching horror movies; going for walks; reading; and most importantly, spending time with friends and family. After graduation, I plan on working at a medium-sized advertising firm.
